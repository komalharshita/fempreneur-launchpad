> **A platform where women learn, build, and launch businesses.**

**The Fempreneur Lab** is a modern web platform designed to empower
women entrepreneurs by providing AI-powered tools, structured learning
paths, curated resources, and a supportive community space.

This project was created for **SheBuilds by Lovable '26** as part of an
initiative to build meaningful technology that supports women in
entrepreneurship.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# ✨ Project Vision

Many women have incredible ideas, creativity, and skills but often lack
the guidance or resources needed to transform those ideas into real
businesses.

**The Fempreneur Lab** helps bridge that gap by providing:

-   AI guidance
-   Beginner-friendly business tools
-   Step-by-step learning paths
-   Curated resources
-   Community inspiration

Our goal is to make entrepreneurship **accessible, supportive, and
achievable** for women everywhere.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🚀 Features

## 🤖 AI Mentor

A friendly AI mentor that acts like a supportive business coach.

Users can ask questions such as: - How do I start a small business? -
How do I price my products? - How can I get my first customers?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🧰 Business Tools

### Business Name Generator

Generate creative brand names and taglines.

### Pricing Calculator

Estimate product pricing based on cost, time, and packaging.

### AI Business Plan Generator

Create simple startup plans for new business ideas.

### Social Media Caption Generator

Generate captions, hashtags, and promotional text for marketing.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📚 Learning Paths

Structured learning journeys designed for beginners:

-   Handmade Product Business
-   Digital Product Business
-   Instagram Store Setup
-   Local Small Business

Each path includes: - Step-by-step guides - Tutorials - Practical
checklists

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 📖 Resource Library

A curated library of helpful resources including:

-   Marketing guides
-   Branding tutorials
-   Design resources
-   Selling platform tutorials
-   Entrepreneurial tips

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 🌟 Women Entrepreneurs Showcase

A community section where women can share and discover inspiring
businesses.

Users can: - Submit their business - Share their journey - Inspire other
women founders

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 🏗 Project Architecture

The platform follows a **multi-page SaaS-style structure**.

    Home
    AI Mentor
    Tools
     ├ Business Name Generator
     ├ Pricing Calculator
     ├ Business Plan Generator
     └ Caption Generator
    Learning Paths
    Resource Library
    Women Entrepreneurs Showcase
